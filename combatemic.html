About Us
COMBATEMIC is a team effort by a group of developers who swore to contribute for the sake of humanity and sustenance. Our mission is to fight back any life-threatening diseases and share knowledge of News and Preventives as soon as possible.

The contribution is from “US” To HUMANITY.

Awareness plays a key role when medicines and treatment are not on around and we here at COMBATEMIC want to provide the best knowledge of what is happening around.

The APP aims to solve most of the problems that individuals are not used to doing and are prone to forget. We don’t believe in being scarred, we believe in the fightback. Make your life simpler and at your fingertips. We want to share this APP for the benefit of society.
